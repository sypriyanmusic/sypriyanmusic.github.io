About Sypriyan

SYPRIYAN

🎵 Official Artist
Spotify • Apple Music • Amazon Music • YouTube

Sypriyan is a Canadian Tamil songwriter, lyricist and music creator. Through cinematic storytelling and heartfelt lyrics, he creates original Tamil songs that connect with listeners around the world. Every song reflects emotion, memory and imagination.

“Every song has a story. Every story has a soul.”

🎵 Original Tamil Music ✍️ Lyrics & Concepts 🎬 Letty Production
